Banana Kheer

This banana kheer is a delicious recipe, which is not fulfill your dessert cravings, you can also have it in your breakfast.

Ingredients of Banana Kheer

  • 2 Cups milk

  • 1 cup mashed banana

  • Sliced banana, to garnish

  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

  • 2 strands kesar (optional)

  • Jaggery powder, as per taste

  • Half cup roughly chopped nuts (of your choice)

How to Make Banana Kheer

  1. Boil the milk on low-medium flame until it thickens.

  2. Add kesar, cardamom powder and dry fruits to it. Simmer until the kesar leaves colour.

  3. Take mashed banana in a bowl and add the milk to it.

  4. Garnish with sliced banana and serve.
