Sindhi Bhori Recipe

Bhori is a traditional Sindhi breakfast recipe, prepared by mashing roti with sugar and butter. This sweet breakfast item is a part of many Sindhis' cherished childhood memories.

Ingredients of Sindhi Bhori

  • 3 tablespoon butter

  • 2 tablespoon sugar

  • 1 freshly cooked whole wheat roti

How to Make Sindhi Bhori

  1. Make a fresh whole-wheat roti. Break the roti into pieces and add them to a bowl.

  2. Add butter and sugar to this and crush and mix all the ingredients with your hands. The butter should melt while mixing.

  3. Serve warm for best taste.
